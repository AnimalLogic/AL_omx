
Why |project|?
==================================
- More user-friendly than Maya's native `om2 <https://help.autodesk.com/view/MAYAUL/2022/ENU/?guid=Maya_SDK_py_ref_namespace_open_maya_html>`_ (aka maya.api.OpenMaya) API.

- Built-in :ref:`undoability`.
